Our Story

In 2015, driven by the desire to bring authentic Asian food to Kuala Lumpur, our founder, Miss Farah Ellysya, along with two Board of Directors, opened Asian food House in KLCC. Rave reviews, and a quick-developing fan base made the culinary visionaries decide to expand their offerings to a more premium venture.

In a space of just six months after the success of Asian food, officially opened its door in Pavillion Bukit Bintang and it has continued to find favor with diners throughout the years, establishing itself as one of the foremost Asian restaurants in the Klang Valley. The same positive response was accorded to Bangkok Jam when it opened in BB Park in 2017.

Hot, Sour, Sweet and
Oh, So Fresh

The popularity of these three Trio’s restaurants birthed the first outlet in IPC Shopping Centre in 2018. The brand’s ‘Hospitality with a Heart’ slogan, along with their stellar food offerings led to the extension over another 2 outlets, with thousands of guests enjoying signature dishes like the popular Grill salmon, Tom Yam Noodles,Asam Pedas,Nasi lemak,Cendol, Chocolate Milkshake and Teh tarik Kaw


All the restaurants in the Group continue with their promise to serve authentic Asian food prepared by chefs from Thailand, using only quality and authentic ingredients from the Land of Smiles.
